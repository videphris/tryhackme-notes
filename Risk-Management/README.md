### Core Concept (In My Own Words)
--- Explain the main idea simply and clearly
Learned qualitative and quantitive methods of risk assessment. Qualitative is more speculative and can be described as high/low urgency based on a chart. Quantitative assessment is more formulaic and numerical. Using formulas to obtain ALEs(Annual Lost Expectancy) and SLEs (Single Loss Expectancy)
Use of formula to calculate if the risk safeguard outweighs the cost, utilizing the formula ALE = Asset Value x EF x ARO before and after applying the safeguard, then subtracting the annual cost of the safeguard.

### Why It Matters
--- Why does this matter? What problem does it solve?
Risk assessment matters to organizations to prevent fines and penalties such as reputation loss. It seeks to create an assessment of the current security scope and policies held to ensure that the organization is operating efficiently.
There are 4 types of risk countermeasures:
Avoid Risk = remove the technology that is causing the risk 
Mitigate Risk = apply measures to decrease the chance of risk
Accept Risk = accept the risk but do it with dilligence and knowledge of what you are accepting
Transfer Risk = get insurance for said risk
